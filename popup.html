What is Twenty?

Twenty is here to help you prevent computervision!

The American Optometric Associaion recommends following the " 20-20-20 rule" to help alleviate digital eyestrain.

Twenty Reminds You

With this extension, you'll get a notification when it's time to take your "twenty" break. You can either close the notification or press the "Take A Break" button on the home tab to indicate that you're starting your break. When twenty seconds are up, the twenty minute timer resumes again.

You can even customize the lengths of your working and break periods in the settings tab.
